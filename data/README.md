# Data description

## tap
- 0 : top
- 1 : left
- 2 : bottom
- 3 : right


## slide
### Top
- 0_0 : top, slide bottom to top
- 0_1 : top, slide right to left
- 0_2 : top, slide top to bottom
- 0_3 : top, slide left to right

### Left
- 1_0 : left, slide bottom to top
- 1_1 : left, slide right to left
- 1_2 : left, slide top to bottom
- 1_3 : left, slide left to right

### Bottom
- 2_0 : bottom, slide bottom to top
- 2_1 : bottom, slide right to left
- 2_2 : bottom, slide top to bottom
- 2_3 : bottom, slide left to right

### Right
- 3_0 : right, slide bottom to top
- 3_1 : right, slide right to left
- 3_2 : right, slide top to bottom
- 3_3 : right, slide left to right
